WEB DESIGN

FACE-TO-FACE COMPETITION
(Team Composition: 2 participants per school)

THE COMPETING ENTRY / TEAM

1. A competing entry shall consist of two (2) students-participants currently and officially enrolled in the school they represent
2. The participants must be assigned by the school / coach / trainer to compete for the Website Design Event.
3. The participants shall have skills and knowledge with HTML, HTML 5, CSS and CSS3 to join in the competition.

DEFINITION OF COACH / TRAINER

1. A full-time teacher/faculty affiliated and appointed by the respective school as a Coach / Trainor / Substitute coach for Website Design competition.
2. A fulltime employee of the respective school either appointed as coach or Substitute for a coach.

CONTEST ATTENDANCE AND ATIRE

1. Attendance Check: Participants must be present at the designated venue at least fifteen (15) minutes before the start of the contest (Elimination and Final Rounds.) Attendance will be promptly recorded at this time.
2. Disqualification Clause: Failure to attend any of the aforementioned events may result in automatic disqualification, along with the forfeiture of any potential awards of prizes.
3. Punctuality Requirement: Failure to arrive before the officail start will result in the forfeiture of the contestant's chance to compete.
